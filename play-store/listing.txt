FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars)
---------------------------------
Pause helps you take back control of your time.

Choose the apps that tend to swallow your evenings — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Reddit, X, Facebook, Snapchat — set how many minutes is "too long," and Pause shows a calm, full-screen reminder when you cross it. A simple beat to ask yourself: do I really want to keep going?

It is not a blocker and it is not about shame. It just interrupts the autopilot for a moment and hands the choice back to you.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Pick exactly which apps to watch — nothing else is ever touched.
• Set your own time limit, including a custom number of minutes.
• Personalise the reminder with your own image and message, or pick a witty preset.
• Runs quietly in the background — no nagging notification to dismiss.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Pause is built to be trustworthy:
• It reads only which app is in the foreground and for how long — never the contents of your screen, your messages, your keystrokes, or your passwords.
• Everything happens on your device. There is no account, no login, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking.
• Nothing Pause sees ever leaves your phone.

HOW IT WORKS
Pause uses Android's "Usage access" to notice when an app you chose has been open long enough, then draws a brief reminder over it. You grant two simple permissions during setup — Usage access and "Display over other apps" — and that's it.

Interrupt the autopilot. Let yourself choose.
