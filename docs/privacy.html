Pause — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pause does not collect, store off your device, or share any personal data. There is no account, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Nothing Pause sees ever leaves your phone.

What Pause does

Pause is a focus tool. When you have been using a social/entertainment app you chose for a set amount of time, Pause shows a brief full-screen reminder so you can decide whether to keep going. All of this happens entirely on your device.

Information Pause accesses (and never sends anywhere)

  • Which app is in the foreground, and for how long. Using Android's Usage access API, Pause reads only the package name of the app currently in front (for example com.instagram.android) and how long it has been open. Pause cannot and does not read the contents of your screen, your messages, your keystrokes, your passwords, or anything you type or view.
  • The list of apps you choose to monitor, and your settings. Stored locally on your device only.
  • A custom reminder image, if you add one. Copied into Pause's private storage on your device. It is never uploaded.

Permissions and why they are used

  • Usage access — to know which app is open and for how long.
  • Display over other apps — to show the reminder on top of the app.
  • Foreground service — keeps the detector running reliably in the background. On Android 13 and newer it runs without any notification (it only appears in the system "Active apps" list); on older versions a minimal, silent entry may appear.

Data sharing and selling

None. Pause has no servers, makes no network requests with your data, and shares nothing with any third party. There is nothing to sell because nothing is collected.

Children

Pause is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.
